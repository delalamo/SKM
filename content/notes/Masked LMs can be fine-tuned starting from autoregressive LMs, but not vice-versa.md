#### Summary
**Masked language models (such as [[BERT]]) can be fine-tuned starting from autoregressive models (such as [[GPT]]) in ways that benefit from scale, but not vice-versa** (Cheng et al 2024[^cheng2024]). This was demonstrated using [[Protein language models|protein language models]].
