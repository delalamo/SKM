## Summary

**[[Protein language models]] are better zero-shot [[Fitness prediction|fitness predictors]] for ranking closely related sequences than more distantly related sequences** (Didi et al 2026 [@didi2026]). This was shown by fine-tuning various sequence-only language models on mutations from mutants in one wildtype sequence, and inferring the effect of mutations from a different wildtype sequence.
