#### Summary
**For [[Inverse folding]], pretraining on all monomeric protein structures prior to fine-tuning on [[Antibodies|antibody]] structures improves sequence recovery from 33.3% to 46.9%** (citation needed). This benefit does not hold if fine-tuning on antibody structures follows pretraining on general [[Protein-protein interactions|protein-protein interaction]] structures.
