#### Summary

**[[Attention (machine learning)|Attention]] matrices of [[Antibody language models]] do not correspond to contacts** (Burbach and [^burbach2024]). This is in contrast to generic [[Protein language models|PLMs]] ([[PLM attention matrices correspond to 3D contacts|link]]).

#### Details

This was determined by fine-tuning [[ESM]]2 on paired [[Antibodies|antibody]] sequences from [[Observable Antibody Space|OAS]].
