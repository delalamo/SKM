#### Summary
**[[Attention (machine learning)|Attention]] matrices of [[Antibodies|antibody]]-specific [[Protein language models|protein language models]] do not correspond to contacts** [^burbach2024]. [[PLM attention matrices correspond to 3D contacts|This is in contrast to generic protein language models]].

#### Details

This was determined by fine-tuning [[ESM]]2 on paired [[Antibodies|antibody]] sequences from [[Observable Antibody Space|OAS]].
