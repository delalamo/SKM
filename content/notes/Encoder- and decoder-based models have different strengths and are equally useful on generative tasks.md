#### Summary
**[[BERT]] models and autoregressive [[Transformer|transformers]] are equally effective on generative tasks and have different strengths and weaknesses related to pretraining** [^samuel2024]. This was observed with [[Disentangled attention|DeBERTa]] and an open-source version of GPT-3.

[^samuel2024]: Samuel (2024) "BERTs are Generative In-Context Learners." NeurIPS. http://papers.nips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/hash/04ea184dfb5f1babb78c093e850a83f9-Abstract-Conference.html
