#### Summary
 **Proteins with greater folding stability have more intermediate partially unfolded states** (Ferrari et al 2025[^ferrari2025]). This was suggested by the observation that average residue-level [[Stability and thermostability|stability]] ($\Delta G_{avg}$) increases sublinearly relative to overall protein stability.
