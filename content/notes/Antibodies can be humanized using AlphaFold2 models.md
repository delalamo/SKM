#### Summary
**Structure-based [[Antibody humanization|humanization]] is possible using models from [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]]** (Tennenhouse et al 2023[^tennenhouse2023]). This suggests that sub-Angstrom accuracy is not necessary for that task.

[^tennenhouse2023]: Tennenhouse et al. (2023) "Computational optimization of antibody humanness and stability by systematic energy-based ranking." *Nature Biomedical Engineering*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41551-023-01079-1
