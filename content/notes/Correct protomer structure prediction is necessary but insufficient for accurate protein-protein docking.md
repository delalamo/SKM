#### Summary
**Correct protomer [[Structure prediction|structure prediction]] is necessary but insufficient for accurate modeling of [[Protein-protein interactions|protein quaternary structure]]** (doi.org/10.64898/2026.04.03.716280). This has also been shown for [[Correct CDRH3 prediction is necessary but insufficient for correct Ab-Ag docking|CDRH3 loops in antibody-antigen complexes]].
