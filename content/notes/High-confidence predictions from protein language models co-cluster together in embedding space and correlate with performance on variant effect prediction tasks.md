#### Summary
**High-confidence predictions (assessed with [[plddt|pLDDT]]) from [[protein-language-models|protein language models]] co-cluster together in embedding space and their placement therein correlates with performance on [[variant-effect-prediction|variant effect prediction]] tasks** [@prabakaran2026]. In contrast, low-confidence predictions fall in a "junkyard".
