**ESM-IF** is an [[inverse-folding|Inverse folding]] method that uses twelve million [[alphafold2|AlphaFold2]] structures during training, in addition to PDB. It uses an encoder-decoder [[Transformer]] architecture with a [[Geometric Vector Perceptrons|GVP]] as well as fixed-order decoding (autoregressive).
