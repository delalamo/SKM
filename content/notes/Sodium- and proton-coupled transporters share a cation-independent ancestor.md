#### Summary
 **Sodium-coupled and proton-coupled [[Secondary-active-transporters]] share a cation-independent ancestor** (Reddy et al 2023[^reddy2023]). This was determined using [[cryo-EM]] and [[Ancestral sequence reconstruction]] and is an example of how functional divergence evolves in unusual ways.
