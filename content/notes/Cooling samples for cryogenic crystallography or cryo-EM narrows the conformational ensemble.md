#### Summary
**Cooling samples for cryogenic [[X-ray-crystallography|X-ray crystallography]] or [[cryo-EM]] narrows the conformational ensemble** (ref 22 of [^rakotoharisoa2023]). This has also been observed using spin labels for (DEER) [^georgieva2012].

[^rakotoharisoa2023]: Rakotoharisoa et al. (2024) "Design of Efficient Artificial Enzymes Using Crystallographically Enhanced Conformational Sampling." Journal of the American Chemical Society. https://doi.org/10.1021/jacs.4c00677
[^georgieva2012]: Georgieva et al. (2012) "Effect of freezing conditions on distances and their distributions derived from Double Electron Electron Resonance (DEER): A study of doubly-spin-labeled T4 lysozyme." *Journal of Magnetic Resonance*. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jmr.2012.01.004
