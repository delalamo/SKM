#### Summary

**[[Sequence perplexity]] and [[TM-score]] are negatively correlated when [[Structure prediction|predicting protein structure]] using [[Protein language models|protein language models]]** (Bushuiev et al 2024[^bushuiev2024]).** This was observed with [[ESMFold]] and [[ESM|ESM3]].
