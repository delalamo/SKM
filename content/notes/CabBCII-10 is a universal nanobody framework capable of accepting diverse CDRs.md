#### Summary
**CabBCII-10 is a universal [[Nanobodies|nanobody]] [[Framework region|framework]] capable of accepting diverse [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRs]] without being destabilized** (see [[Humanization can destabilize antibodies]]; Vincke et al 2009[^vincke2009]).

[^vincke2009]: Vincke et al. (2009) "General Strategy to Humanize a Camelid Single-domain Antibody and Identification of a Universal Humanized Nanobody Scaffold." *Journal of Biological Chemistry*. https://doi.org/10.1074/jbc.m806889200
