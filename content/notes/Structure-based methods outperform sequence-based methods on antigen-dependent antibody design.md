#### Summary

**Structure-based methods such as [[Inverse folding|inverse folding]] methods outperform sequence-based methods such as [[Protein language models|PLMs]] on the design of high-affinity [[Antibodies|antibodies]]** (Shanker et al 2024[^shanker2024]). This is irrespective of whether the language model was trained on [[Antibody language models|antibodies]] (AbLang) or [[Protein language models|generic proteins]] ([[ESM]]-1v).
