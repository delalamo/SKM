#### Summary

**Structure-based methods such as [[tags/inverse-folding|inverse folding]] methods outperform sequence-based methods such as [[tags/protein-language-models|PLMs]] on the design of high-affinity [[tags/antibodies|antibodies]]** [@shanker2024]. This is irrespective of whether the language model was trained on [[tags/antibodies|antibodies]] (AbLang) or [[tags/protein-language-models|generic proteins]] ([[ESM]]-1v).
