#### Summary

**Synthetic [[Multiple sequence alignments|MSAs]] outperform single-sequence inference in MSA-based [[Structure prediction]]** (Wells et al 2025a[^wells2025]). This was demonstrated using ProFam1 and [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]].

[^wells2025]: Wells et al. (2025) "ProFam: Open-Source Protein Family Language Modelling for Fitness Prediction and Design." https://doi.org/10.64898/2025.12.19.695431
