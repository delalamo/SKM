## Summary

**[[Protein language models]] can predict zero-shot which proteins belong to the same species** ([Hallee et al 2025](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.10.07.681002)). This is used to cheat when predicting whether [[Protein-protein interactions|two proteins interact]].
