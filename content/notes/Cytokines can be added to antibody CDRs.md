#### Summary
**[[Cytokines]] can be added to [[Antibodies|antibody]] [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRs]] while maintaining correct folding of both components** [^didonato2025]. Here, a monomerized IL-10 cytokine was added to the [[Complementarity-determining regions#CDRL1|CDRL1]] of Palivizumab, although all six were tested.

[^didonato2025]: DiDonato et al. (2025) "A novel interleukin-10 antibody graft to treat inflammatory bowel disease." *Structure*. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.str.2024.12.010
