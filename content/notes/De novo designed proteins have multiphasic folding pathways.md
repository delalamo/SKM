#### Summary
**[[Protein backbone design|De novo designed proteins]] have complex, multiphasic [[Protein folding|folding]] pathways not found in naturally occurring proteins** [@watters2007]. This was observed using ([[NMR]]) and stopped-flow kinetics on the designed protein Top7. The authors conclude that [[Smooth protein folding landscapes are the result of natural selection|smooth protein folding landscapes are the product of natural selection]].
