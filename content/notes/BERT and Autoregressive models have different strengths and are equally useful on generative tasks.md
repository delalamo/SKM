#### Summary
**[[BERT]] models and autoregressive [[Transformer|transformers]] are equally effective on generative tasks and have different strengths and weaknesses related to pretraining** (Samuel 2024[^samuel2024]). This was observed with [[Disentangled attention|DeBERTa]] and an open-source version of GPT-3.

[^samuel2024]: Samuel (2024) "BERTs are Generative In-Context Learners." https://doi.org/10.48550/ARXIV.2406.04823
