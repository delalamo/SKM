#### Summary

About two thirds (68.8%) of deletion mutations not tolerated by proteins, whereas tolerance to insertion mutations varied across proteins (more tolerated in six of nine proteins tested by authors; [10.1101__2023.10.06.561180|Topolska et al 2023]). This is also true of out-of-frame insertions and deletions, which introduce a substitution. Tolerance here is measured in abundance (see [[Protein stability, expression, and resistance to proteolysis are correlated]])
