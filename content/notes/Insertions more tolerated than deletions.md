#### Summary

About two thirds (68.8%) of deletion mutations not tolerated by proteins, whereas tolerance to insertion mutations varied across proteins (more tolerated in six of nine proteins tested by authors; [^topolska2023]). This is also true of out-of-frame insertions and deletions, which introduce a substitution. Tolerance here is measured in abundance (see [[Protein stability, expression, and resistance to proteolysis are correlated]])

[^topolska2023]: Topolska et al. (2023) "Deep indel mutagenesis reveals the impact of amino acid insertions and deletions on protein stability and function." https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.10.06.561180
