#### Summary

**Most mutations are destabilizing, and most of those that aren't are located on the surfaces of enzymes** (Tokuriki et al 2008[^tokuriki2008]). The corollary is that most mutations on the surface have no effect; but this is not true of [[Variant effect prediction#Indels|indels]] (Topolska et al 2023[^topolska2023]). Green fluorescent protein is an example where most mutations that affected function (fluorescence) were pointing inward; i.e., not on the protein surface (Sarkisyan et al 2016[^sarkisyan2016]).
