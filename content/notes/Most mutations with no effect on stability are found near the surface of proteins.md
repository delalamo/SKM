#### Summary

**Most mutations are destabilizing, and most of those that aren't are located on the surfaces of enzymes** [@tokuriki2008]. The corollary is that most mutations on the surface have no effect; but this is not true of [[Variant effect prediction#Indels|indels]] [@topolska2023]. Green fluorescent protein is an example where most mutations that affected function (fluorescence) were pointing inward; i.e., not on the protein surface [@sarkisyan2016].
