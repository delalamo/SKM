#### Summary

**[[AlphaFold3]] [[TM-score|ipTM]] can distinguish [[Antibodies|antibodies]] that bind and those that don't with an [[Binary classifiers|AUC]] of 0.86** [@bennett2024]. However, previous studies have not found the same for AlphaFold2-generation models [@loureno2024]. Meanwhile fine-tuned [[RosettaFold]] was also unable to distinguish these, suggesting a very high base level of performance is required to distinguish binders and nonbinders.
