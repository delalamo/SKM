#### Summary
**[[Antibodies]] and [[Nanobodies|nanobodies]] can de-symmetrize large symmetric proteins for [[cryo-EM]] studies.** Hofmann et al 2019[^hofmann2019] used a conformationally non-selective nanobody to de-symmetrize the large primary active transporter TmrAB to assist with cryo-EM.

[^hofmann2019]: Hofmann et al. (2019) "Conformation space of a heterodimeric ABC exporter under turnover conditions." *Nature*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-019-1391-0
