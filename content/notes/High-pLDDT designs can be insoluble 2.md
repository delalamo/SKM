#### Summary

High-pLDDT designs, particularly those generated by hallucination, can be insoluble ([10.1126__science.add2187|Dauparas et al 2022] and [10.1126__science.abn2100|Wang et al 2022]). [10.1002__pro.4653|Goverde et al 2023] found that inversion of AlphaFold2 led to many surface-exposed hydrophobic residues, and that manual post-design intervention led to in vitro expression of some designs. Alternatively, inverse folding can circumvent this (see [[Inverse folding methods outperform sequence design by hallucination]]).
