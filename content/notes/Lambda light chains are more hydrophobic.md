#### Summary
**Several reports have stated that [[tags/antibodies|Antibodies]] with [[tags/light-chains|kappa light chains]] are less [[tags/antibody-developability|hydrophobic]] than those with lambda light chains** (references 11, 12, 17, and 18 of [@raybould2023]). However, [@shehata2019] found that this was not the case.
