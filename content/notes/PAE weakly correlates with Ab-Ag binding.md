## Summary

**The [[Predicted aligned error|PAE]] of [[AlphaFold]] weakly correlates with the binding affinity of [[Antibodies]] to their antigens** ([Jin et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.12.10.570461)). It is more effective at discriminating between positive and negative [[Trastuzumab]] binders.
