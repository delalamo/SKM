#### Summary

**[[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] can be fine-tuned on specific protein families by adding [[Evoformer]] layers** (Glukhov et al 2023[^glukhov2023]). They added two layers and fine-tuned on [[Major histocompatibility complex I|MHC-I]] structures.
