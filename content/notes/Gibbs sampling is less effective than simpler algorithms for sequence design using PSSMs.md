#### Summary
 **When designing [[Single chain variable fragments]], Gibbs sampling was found to be less effective than either genetic algorithms or simple greedy hill-climbing** (fitness functions were either PSSMs, [[BERT]] language models, or [[Gaussian processes]]; [@li2023machine]).
