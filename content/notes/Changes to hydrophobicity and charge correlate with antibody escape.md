#### Summary
**Methods that monitor changes in [[Developability|hydrophobicity]] or charge of [[Variant effect prediction|missense mutations]] in antigens are better predictors of antibody escape than either metric in isolation or the [[BLOSUM62]] matrix** (Thadani et al 2023[^thadani2023]).

[^thadani2023]: Thadani et al. (2023) "Learning from prepandemic data to forecast viral escape." *Nature*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-023-06617-0
