#### Summary
**Multiplexed [[Ancestral sequence reconstruction|ASR]] involves the generation of many possible phylogenetic trees in parallel.** It was used by [^matthews2023] to train small [[Protein language models|PLMs]].

[^matthews2023]: Matthews et al. (2024) "Leveraging ancestral sequence reconstruction for protein representation learning." Nature Machine Intelligence. https://doi.org/10.1038/s42256-024-00935-2
