#### Summary
**[[AlphaFold2]] predicts ground states more often** (Chakravarty and [^chakravarty2022]). In a benchmark of [[Fold-switching proteins]], AlphaFold2 predicted the ground state 70% of the time, vs the excited state 30% of the time.
