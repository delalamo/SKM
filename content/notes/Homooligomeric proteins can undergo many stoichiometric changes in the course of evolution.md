#### Summary
**Homo-oligomeric proteins can undergo many changes to stoichiometry in the course of [[Evolution and natural selection|evolution]]** [@sendker2024]. This was observed across citrate synthases, which are functionally highly conserved by nonetheless observed to exist as dimers, hexamers, octamers, etc.
