#### Summary

Guidance potentials can be added to diffusion-based structure prediction methods for enhanced sampling of protein dynamics ([10.64898__2026.02.10.704873|Lam et al 2026], [10.64898__2025.12.17.693105|Ohnuki & Okazaki 2025]). However, the results do not necessarily reproduce the effectiveness of MSA subsampling.
