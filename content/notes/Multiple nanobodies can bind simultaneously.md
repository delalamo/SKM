#### Summary
**Multiple [[Nanobodies|nanobodies]] can bind their targets simultaneously and can bind in a synergistic manner.** Mast et al 2021[^mast2021] demonstrated this with the SARS-CoV-2 [[Spike protein|Spike protein]].
