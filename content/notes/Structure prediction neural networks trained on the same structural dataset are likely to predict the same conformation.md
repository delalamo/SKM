#### Summary
**[[Structure prediction]] neural networks trained on the same structural dataset are likely to predict the same conformation, even when the training hyperparameters differ** This was shown by the fact that the five released [[Alphafold2]] models all tend to model the same conformational state of fold-switching proteins[^chakravarty2022].

[^chakravarty2022]: Chakravarty & Porter (2022) "AlphaFold2 fails to predict protein fold switching." *Protein Science*. https://doi.org/10.1002/pro.4353
