#### Summary
**[[tags/structure-prediction|Structure prediction]] neural networks trained on the same structural dataset are likely to predict the same conformation, even when the training hyperparameters differ** This was shown by the fact that the five released [[tags/alphafold2|Alphafold2]] models all tend to model the same conformational state of fold-switching proteins [@chakravarty2022].
