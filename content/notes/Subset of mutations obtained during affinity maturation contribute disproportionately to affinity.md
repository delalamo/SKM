#### Summary
**A subset of mutations contribute disproportionately to affinity** [@julian2017; @kirby2025]. Using [[Display methods|yeast display]], [@kirby2025] found that most mutations obtained during [[tags/affinity-maturation|affinity maturation]] have no impact on affinity. These may have instead been selected to [[Affinity maturation also selects for lower self-association|lower self-association to other human proteins]].
