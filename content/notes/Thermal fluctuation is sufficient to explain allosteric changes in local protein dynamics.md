#### Summary
 **Thermal fluctuation is sufficient to explain (allosteric changes) in local protein dynamics** [^cooper1984]. This is in contrast to a "domino model" of propagation of effects as proposed by [^monod1965].

#### Details
Per [^wankowicz2024]:
> "proteins and other biological macromolecules may have evolved to take functional advantage not only of mean conformational states but also of the inevitable thermal fluctuations about the mean."
