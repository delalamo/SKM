#### Summary
Subnetworks within [[tags/protein-language-models|protein language models]] encode specific protein families [@vinod2026], and individual competencies are stored in distinct parts of the neural network. This might be why [[Sequence-only protein language models implicitly cluster proteins at fineness levels that increase with size|larger PLMs are able to cluster protein families are finer levels]], as their capacity is simply larger.
