#### Summary

**[[MSA Transformer]] generates more realistic and diverse sequences than [[Potts models]]** (Sgarbossa et al 2023[^sgarbossa2023]). They are also more likely to fold to a high [[TM-score]] (Alamdari et al 2023[^alamdari2023]).
