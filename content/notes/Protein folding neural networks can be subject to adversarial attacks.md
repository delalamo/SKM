## Summary

**[[Structure prediction#Methods|Protein folding neural networks]] can be subject to adversarial attacks.** Small, calculated changes in AA sequence via adversarial attacks can lead to huge RMSD changes in [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] and [[RosettaFold]], and there are strategies to identify these ([Jha et al 2021](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2109.04460), [Alkhouri et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2301.04093), [Yuan et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2305.08929)). This is compared to adversarial attacks in vision NNs. It is unclear how correlated to weaknesses of these NNs are.
