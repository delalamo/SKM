## Summary

**Compression of protein sequences and [[Structure prediction|structures]] from [[Protein language models#Representations|PLM representations]] passed through [[Variational autoencoders|VQ-VAEs]] is prone to codebook collapse when insufficiently compressed** ([Lu et al 2024b][^lu2024]). This can be ameliorated using alternative architectures.
