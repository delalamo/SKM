#### Summary

**Discrete [[Protein structure tokenization|structural tokens]] are worse than continuous ML-derived representations for calculating structural alignments** [@trinquier2025]. In this case, the tokens were from [[Foldseek]] and a related method, while the continuous representations were from a retrained [[ProteinMPNN]] encoder.
