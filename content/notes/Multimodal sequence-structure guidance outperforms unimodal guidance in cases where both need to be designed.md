#### Summary
**In cases where [[Protein Design|protein design]] requires both sequence and structure to be designed (such as disulfide design, or cation-$\pi$ design, simultaneous [[Diffusion guidance|guidance]] of both sequence and structure outperforms guidance of just one modality at a time [^rectorbrooks2026]**.
