#### Summary

**[[MD simulations]] reproduce experimental [[Stability and thermostability|stability]] values to within about 1kcal/mol** (Lewis et al 2024[^lewis2024]). This is estimated by comparing long trajectories from the same protein across different force fields.

[^lewis2024]: Lewis et al. (2024) "Scalable emulation of protein equilibrium ensembles with generative deep learning." https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.12.05.626885
