#### Summary

**[[MD simulations]] reproduce experimental [[Stability and thermostability|stability]] values to within about 1kcal/mol** [^lewis2024]. This is estimated by comparing long trajectories from the same protein across different force fields.

[^lewis2024]: Lewis et al. (2025) "Scalable emulation of protein equilibrium ensembles with generative deep learning." Science. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.adv9817
