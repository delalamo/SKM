## Summary

**[[Protein language models]] learn structure-level features, including [[Intrinsically disordered regions|disorder]], in later layers** ([Adams et al 2025](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.02.06.636901)). This was shown using [[Sparse autoencoder|sparse autoencoders]].
