#### Summary
**Weighting training sequences by fitness improves identification of other fit sequences**[^zhu2024]. This was shown when training linear models and two-layer MLPs for design of Adeno-associated virus capsid proteins, significantly improving prediction of fit sequences compared to an unweighed dataset.
