#### Summary
 **[[Evolution and natural selection#Mutations|Mutations]] that are beneficial for enzymes that are far from the active site tune [[Protein dynamics|conformational dynamics]] to improve substrate binding and substrate release** (Zarifi et al 2025[^zarifi2025]). However, they do not "compensate" for destabilizing active site mutations as was previously hypothesized. This was shown using [[Kemp eliminase]].
