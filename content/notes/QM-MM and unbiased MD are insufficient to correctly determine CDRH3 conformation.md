#### Summary
**Neither QM-MM nor unbiased [[MD simulations|MD]] can consistently sample the correct [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] conformation more accurately than [[Structure prediction|structure prediction]] neural networks like [[AlphaFold]]** (Chen et al 2024a[^chen2024]).

[^chen2024]: Chen et al. (2024) "Accurate prediction of CDR-H3 loop structures of antibodies with deep learning." *eLife*. https://doi.org/10.7554/eLife.91512.4
