#### Summary
**[[B cells|B cell]] proliferation during [[Affinity maturation|affinity maturation]] is driven by number of bound antigen molecules, which is in turn dictated by [[Developability|expression]], trafficking, [[Stability and thermostability|thermostability]], and binding affinity** (Tharp et al 2026[^tharp2026]).
