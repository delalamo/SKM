#### Summary

Of ~350 screened mutations in the [[Intrinsically disordered regions|intrinsically disordered regions]] of 80 proteins shown to be pathogenic, half were shown to either enhance [[protein-protein-interactions|protein-protein interactions]] or create new interactions, while the other half shown to diminish binding [@kliche2023].
