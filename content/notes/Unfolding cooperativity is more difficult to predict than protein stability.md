#### Summary
 **[[tags/protein-folding|Protein unfolding cooperativity]] (whether a protein unfolds all at once or independently in different regions) is more difficult to predict than [[tags/thermostability|protein stability]]** [@ferrari2025]. Even models predicting intra-family differences could only achieve correlations of 0.16-0.24, compared to 0.40-0.53 for stability when the same input features were used. [[tags/protein-language-models|Protein language models]] were especially inept at predicting these.
