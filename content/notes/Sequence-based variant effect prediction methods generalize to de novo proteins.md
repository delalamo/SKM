**Sequence-based ML models for [[tags/variant-effect-prediction|variant effect prediction]] generalize to *[[tags/protein-backbone-design|de novo]]* designed proteins** [@marquet2024]. A [[tags/protein-language-models|PLM]] with a top-off layer that was fine-tuned on bulk GEMME predictions achieved a Spearman correlation of 0.386 on *de novo* proteins in the mega-scale dataset [@tsuboyama2023].
