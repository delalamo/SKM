#### Summary
**High-abundance enzymes tend to be comprised of amino acids that are less costly to synthesize, particularly on the protein surface** (Lemke et al 2025[^lemke2025] and refs 14-16 therein).

[^lemke2025]: Lemke et al. (2025) "The role of metabolism in shaping enzyme structures over 400 million years." *Nature*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-025-09205-6
