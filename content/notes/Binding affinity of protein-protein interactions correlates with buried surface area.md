#### Summary
**The binding affinity between [[Protein-protein interactions|two proteins]] correlates with buried surface area** (Chen et al 2013[^chen2013]. At least 500 $Å^{2}$ are required for a stable complex to form (Day et al 2012[^day2012]), and the effect levels off at >2000 $Å^{2}$ (Chen et al 2013[^chen2013]).
