**Grokking** refers to training a neural network far beyond overfitting. [[10.48550__ARXIV.2405.15071|Wang et al 2024]] showed that it can improve reasoning abilities of [[Transformer|transformers]], whereas [Springer et al 2025](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/springer25a.html) showed [[Overtrained language models are more difficult to fine-tune|it can make models more difficult to fine-tune]].
