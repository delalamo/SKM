**Grokking** refers to training a neural network far beyond overfitting. Wang et al. [@wang2024b] showed that it can improve reasoning abilities of [[Transformer|transformers]], whereas Springer et al. [@springer2025] showed [[Overtrained language models are more difficult to fine-tune|it can make models more difficult to fine-tune]].
