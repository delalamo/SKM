#### Summary
**[[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] mediates pairing between [[Heavy chains|heavy]] and [[Light chains|light]] chains of [[Antibodies|antibodies]] by making contacts with the [[Framework region|framework region]] of the opposite chain** [^dudzic2024].
