#### Summary
**Most [[Nanobodies|camelid VHHs]] have high sequence identity with human IGHV3, sometimes up to 95% identity in the [[Framework region|framework]]** (Rossotti et al 2021[^rossotti2021]). This is comparable to murine-derived clinical [[Antibodies|antibodies]] such as [[Trastuzumab]]. Despite this, they still elicit [[Immunogenicity|immunogenic]] reactions.
