#### Summary
 **Predicted protein structures are less designable than experimental models in ways that suggest distortions at the local but not global level** (Lin et al 2024[^lin2024]). This was observed by designing predicted models and attempting to forward-fold them, and could explain why [[Training inverse folding and diffusion models exclusively on predicted protein structures worsens performance due to how locally perfect they are|training inverse folding methods exclusively on predicted models worsens performance relative to including experimental structures]].
