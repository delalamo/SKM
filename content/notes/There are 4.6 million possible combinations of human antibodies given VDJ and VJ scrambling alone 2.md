#### Summary
**There are 4.6 million possible human [[Antibodies|antibody]] combinations of V/D/J genes across heavy and light chains** [^rees2020]. This is calculated from the number of VH, DH, JH, VK, JK, VL, and JL genes in the proteome.

[^rees2020]: Rees (2020) "Understanding the human antibody repertoire." *mAbs*. https://doi.org/10.1080/19420862.2020.1729683
