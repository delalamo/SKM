#### Summary

**Conformational changes have been modeled using [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] by [[Inversion of protein folding neural networks|inversion]] and driving specific [[Collective variables|collective variables]]** (Xie et al 2023[^xie2023]). A related approach using [[ESMFold]] attempted to sample conformational transition paths using language model embeddings as the target (del Alamo et al 2023[^del2023]).

[^xie2023]: Xie et al. (2023) "Conditioned Protein Structure Prediction." https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.09.24.559171
[^del2023]: del Alamo et al. (2023) "Conformational sampling and interpolation using language-based protein folding neural networks." https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.12.16.571997
