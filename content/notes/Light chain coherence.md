#### Summary
**Light chain coherence refers to the high probability that functional antibodies expressed by memory [[b-cells|B cells]] with the same [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] loop and [[Germline|V-gene]] also share the same [[light-chains|light chain]]** [@jaffe2022]. The finding holds across pairs of B-cells with the same heavy V-gene and CDRH3 length from different donors, and across different datasets collected at different times.
