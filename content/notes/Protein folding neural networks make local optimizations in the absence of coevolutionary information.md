## Summary

**[[Structure prediction|Protein folding neural networks]] make local optimizations in the absence of coevolutionary information** ([Gut et al 2024](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioadv/vbae187)). This was observed using [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] and includes side chain optimizations and resolution of steric clashes. The observation is consistent with the hypothesis put forth by [Roney & Ovchinnikov](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.129.238101) that they learn an energy function but require evolutionary info to traverse the structural space.
