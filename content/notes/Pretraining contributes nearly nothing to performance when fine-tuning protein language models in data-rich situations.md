## Summary

**Pretraining contributes nearly nothing to performance when fine-tuning [[Protein language models|protein language models]] under data-rich conditions** ([Didi et al 2026][^didi2026]). This was determined by fine-tuning both pretrained models and randomly initialized equivalents on the same dataset.
