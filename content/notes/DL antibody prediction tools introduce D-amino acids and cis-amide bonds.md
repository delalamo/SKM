#### Summary
**Deep learning tools for [[Antibodies|antibody]] structure prediction consistently produce D-amino acids, cis-amide bonds, and steric clashes** (Fernandez-Quintero et al 2023[^fernandezquintero2023]). The tools evaluated include AbLooper, DeepAb, [[IgFold]], [[ImmuneBuilder]], and MOE homology modeler. Only DeepAb and ImmuneBuilder did not introduce D-amino acids.
