#### Summary
**Lower [[Antibodies|antibody]] [[Affinity maturation|affinity]] is desirable when target antigens are present in non-tumor tissues, albeit in lower concentrations** (on-target off-tumor targeting; Weisser et al 2023[^weisser2023]).

[^weisser2023]: Weisser et al. (2023) "An anti-HER2 biparatopic antibody that induces unique HER2 clustering and complement-dependent cytotoxicity." *Nature Communications*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-37029-3
