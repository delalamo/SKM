#### Summary
**Amino acid propensities in the proteome correlate with differences in metabolic flux** (Krick et al 2014[^krick2014]). The most abundant amino acid (leucine) is 10x more common in proteins than least abundant (cysteine), and differences like this could be explained by metabolic cost alone.
