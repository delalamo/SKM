#### Summary
**[[Contrastive learning]] allows functional protein sequence (clusters) to be effectively segregated and improves protein [[Function prediction]]** (Yu et al 2023a[^yu2023]). Segregation of EC numbers in the embedding space was greater compared to the starting (ESM)-1b model.
