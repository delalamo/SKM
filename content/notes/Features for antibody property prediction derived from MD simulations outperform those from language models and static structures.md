#### Summary
**[[Developability]] properties of [[Antibodies|antibodies]], such as [[Stability and thermostability|thermostability]], can be predicted with higher accuracy using features derived from [[MD simulations]] compared to those obtained from either static structures or [[Antibody language models|language model embeddings]]** (Rollins et al 2024[^rollins2024]). Bashour et al 2024[^bashour2024] found that developability predictions made from trajectories starting from either experimental structures or [[Antibody structure prediction|predicted structures]] tended to correlate with each other better than predictions made from just the experimental structures or predicted structures.
