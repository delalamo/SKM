#### Summary
 **The [[AlphaFold]] and [[ESMFold]] databases can be mined for alternate conformations** (Wayment-Steele et al 2023[^waymentsteele2023], Herrington et al 2023[^herrington2023]). This is because different sub-families of sequences yield different conformational states.

[^waymentsteele2023]: Wayment-Steele et al. (2023) "Predicting multiple conformations via sequence clustering and AlphaFold2." *Nature*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-023-06832-9
[^herrington2023]: Schwartz et al. (2024) "Alternating Access of a Neurotransmitter:Sodium Symporter Bacterial Homolog Determined from AlphaFold2 Ensembles and DEER Spectroscopy." https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.04.08.587957
