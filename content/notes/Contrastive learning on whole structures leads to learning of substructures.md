#### Summary

**Contrastive learning on structures of antibody Fvs leads to an ability to distinguish specific structural clusters of CDRs** (Singh et al 2023[^singh2023]). Authors attempted to get cosine similarity to match TM-score between antibody structures. See [[Template CDR retrieval using embeddings]].

[^singh2023]: Singh et al. (2023) "Learning the Language of Antibody Hypervariability." https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.04.26.538476
