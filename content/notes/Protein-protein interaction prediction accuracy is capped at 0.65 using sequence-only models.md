#### Summary

**[[Protein-protein interactions#Prediction|Protein-protein interaction prediction]] accuracy is capped at 0.65 using [[Protein language models|sequence-only models]], regardless of fine-tuning scheme or model scale** [^reim2025][^ahmed2025]. This was observed in two studies: one using exclusively the [[ESM]] family of models, the other combining [[Antibody language models|antibody language models]] and generalist language models to predict epitopes.

[^reim2025]: Reim et al. (2025) "Deep learning models for unbiased sequence-based PPI prediction plateau at an accuracy of 0.65." https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.01.23.634454
[^ahmed2025]: Ahmed et al. (2025) "Improved Graph-Based Antibody-Aware Epitope Prediction with Protein Language Model-Based Embeddings." https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.02.12.637989
