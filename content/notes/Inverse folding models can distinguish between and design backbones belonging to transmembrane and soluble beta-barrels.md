#### Summary
 **[[inverse-folding|Inverse folding]] methods can successfully distinguish between membrane and soluble beta barrels, and can design them with hydrophobic and hydrophilic residues in the right places** [@dolorfino2024]. They demonstrated this with [[ProteinMPNN]].

#### Details
Before this preprint was released, ProteinMPNN was said to fail at designing transmembrane beta barrels in Hermosilla et al.'s NeurIPS 2023 talk, despite designing sequences that pass all tests; the presenter suspected it was because successful designs do not occupy the ground state, which is an aggregated state.
