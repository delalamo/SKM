## Summary

**Optimal transport outperforms mean-pooling on protein property prediction** ([NaderiAlizadeh & Singh 2024](https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.01.29.577794)). This uses a learnable aggregation function to convert a variable-length vector generated by a [[Protein language models|protein language model]] into a fixed-length vector used for inference.
