## Summary

**Optimal transport outperforms mean-pooling on protein property prediction** (NaderiAlizadeh & Singh 2024 [@naderializadeh2024]). This uses a learnable aggregation function to convert a variable-length vector generated by a [[Protein language models|protein language model]] into a fixed-length vector used for inference.
