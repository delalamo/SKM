## Summary

**Protein models designed by [[Inverse folding|inverse folding]] can be used to augment datasets of natural sequences when training [[Protein language models|protein language models]] and [[Structure prediction|protein folding neural networks]]** ([Hayes et al 2025][^hayes2025]). This was done for [[ESM]]3, although no ablation data is available to show its impact.
