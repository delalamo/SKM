## Summary

**Protein models designed by [[inverse-folding|inverse folding]] can be used to augment datasets of natural sequences when training [[protein-language-models|protein language models]] and [[structure-prediction|protein folding neural networks]]** [@hayes2025; @wells2025profam]. This was demonstrated using ProFam1 and [[alphafold2|AlphaFold2]], with inference on synthetic MSAs outperforming single-sequence predictions. Similar results were also presented with [[ESM]]3, although no ablation data is available to show its impact.
