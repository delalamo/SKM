#### Summary
**Training on updated databases (such as UniRef) does not guarantee better performance of language models and [[tags/protein-language-models|protein language models]]** [@fournier2024]. The largest performance boost observed by [@fournier2024] when training PLMs on UniRef100 coincided with an unusually large culling of data from training databases.
