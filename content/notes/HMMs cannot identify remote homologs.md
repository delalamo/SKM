#### Summary
**Remote homologs with sequence identity in the 20-25% region cannot be identified using methods for assembling [[Multiple sequence alignments]] centered on hidden Markov models** (Kilinc et al 2023[^kilinc2023]). This was corroborated by Liu et al 2024a[^liu2024]. Wu et al 2024[^wu2024] found that [[Protein language models|PLMs]] were better than HMMs at homolog detection at all scales ([[Larger PLMs are better at homolog detection|link]]).

[^kilinc2023]: Kilinc et al. (2023) "Improved global protein homolog detection with major gains in function identification." *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences*. https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2211823120
[^liu2024]: Liu et al. (2024) "PLMSearch: Protein language model powers accurate and fast sequence search for remote homology." *Nature Communications*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-46808-5
[^wu2024]: Wu et al. (2024) "ProteinCLIP: enhancing protein language models with natural language." https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.05.14.594226
