#### Summary
**Grafting loops involved in catalysis from one enzyme to another can lead to changes in ligand specificity** [^corbella2023]. For example, transferring loops from chymotrypsin to trypsin yielded chymotrypsin-like activity profile, and something similar was observed with luciferase (see Corbella's citations for this).

[^corbella2023]: Corbella et al. (2023) "Loop dynamics and the evolution of enzyme activity." *Nature Reviews Chemistry*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41570-023-00495-w
