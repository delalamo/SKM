#### Summary
**Fast [[Protein dynamics|dynamic processes]] in proteins contribute to slower conformational changes** (Henzler-Wildman et al 2007[^henzlerwildman2007]). This was shown in the hinge region of adenylate kinase, which drives the open-closed conformational change.

[^henzlerwildman2007]: Henzler-Wildman et al. (2007) "A hierarchy of timescales in protein dynamics is linked to enzyme catalysis." *Nature*. https://doi.org/10.1038/nature06407
