#### Summary
**Sequence recovery in [[Inverse folding|inverse folding]] models is not correlated with self-consistency of generated designs, which is defined as the recovery of the target fold when predicting with a [[Structure prediction|structure prediction]] method** (Birnbaum & Keating 2026[^birnbaum2026]).
