#### Summary
**Closely-related enzymes can have disparate catalytic efficiencies, and their values correlate only weakly with sequence similarity** (Muir et al 2024[^muir2024]). For example, adenylate kinase $k_{cat}$ values span three orders of magnitude. This makes it difficult for [[Protein language models|protein language models]] to effectively [[Protein language model embeddings are more predictive of homology than catalytic efficiency|predict catalytic properties in a zero-shot setting]].
