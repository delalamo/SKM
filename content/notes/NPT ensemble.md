#### Summary
The **NPT ensemble** (isothermal-isobaric ensemble) maintains number of particles, pressure, and temperature in a [[MD simulations|molecular dynamics simulation]]. Spontaneous changes in an NPT ensemble cannot lead to increases in [[Gibbs free energy]] (Frenkel & Smit 2002[^frenkel2002]). The reservoir acts as both a thermostat and a barostat.

[^frenkel2002]: Unknown (2002) "Understanding Molecular Simulation." https://doi.org/10.1016/B978-0-12-267351-1.X5000-7
