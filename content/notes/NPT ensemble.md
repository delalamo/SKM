#### Summary
The **NPT ensemble** (isothermal-isobaric ensemble) maintains number of particles, pressure, and temperature in a [[MD simulations|molecular dynamics simulation]]. Spontaneous changes in an NPT ensemble cannot lead to increases in [[Gibbs free energy]] [@frenkel2002]. The reservoir acts as both a thermostat and a barostat.
