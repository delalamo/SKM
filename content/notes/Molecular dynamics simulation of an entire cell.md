#### Summary
 **The minimal cell JCVI-syn3A, which has 500 genes, has been modeled with [[Coarse-grained]] [[MD simulations]]** (Stevens et al 2023[^stevens2023]). The entire system had six billion atoms.

[^stevens2023]: Stevens et al. (2023) "Molecular dynamics simulation of an entire cell." *Frontiers in Chemistry*. https://doi.org/10.3389/fchem.2023.1106495
