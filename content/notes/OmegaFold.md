**OmegaFold** is a [[structure-prediction|protein structure prediction]] method from Wu et al. [@wu2022] that uses a custom-designed [[protein-language-models|masked PLM]] to predict structures without [[Multiple sequence alignments]]. Similar to [[ESMFold]]. Found to model [[Complementarity-determining regions#CDRH3|CDRH3]] loops more accurately than [[alphafold2|AlphaFold2]] [@singh2025].
