#### Summary

**[[Protein dynamics#Ensemble prediction|Protein ensemble prediction]] methods do not generate conformations that can be used for [[protein-protein-interactions|PPI]] docking** [@kyrylenko2025]. This was even the case when the resulting structures were closer in RMSD than the alternative conformation (e.g., the apo unbound state normally being predicted).
