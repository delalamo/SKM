#### Summary

**Monomeric [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] is better able to distinguish positive and negative [[Protein-protein interactions|PPIs]] than AF2-multimer** (Zhang et al 2024a[^zhang2024]). Pacesa et al 2025[^pacesa2025] found that this made it effective at filtering *de novo* binder designs made by [[Inversion of protein folding neural networks|hallucination]].
