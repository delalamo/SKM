#### Summary
**In [[Antibodies|antibodies]] during [[Affinity maturation|affinity maturation]], antigen-binding affinity and broad specificity present competing objectives, with the former achieved by rigidification and the latter by maintained flexibility while targeting conserved epitopes** (Ovchinnikov et al 2018[^ovchinnikov2018]). A quantitative model captures some degrees of the trade-off between these two.

[^ovchinnikov2018]: Ovchinnikov et al. (2018) "Role of framework mutations and antibody flexibility in the evolution of broadly neutralizing antibodies." *eLife*. https://doi.org/10.7554/eLife.33038
