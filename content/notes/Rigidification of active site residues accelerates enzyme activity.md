#### Summary
**Rigidification of active site residues accelerates enzyme activity.** This was shown in a [[Kemp eliminase]] where residues in the first and second shell surrounding the active site were mutated [@broom2020; @zarifi2025], as well as synthetic *de novo* designed enzymes [@hou2025].
