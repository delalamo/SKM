#### Summary

**[[ESMFold]] [[pLDDT]] correlates with intra-family differences in protein [[Stability and thermostability|stability]] and experimental success (expression, monomeric, etc), but not inter-family differences** (Ferrari et al 2025[^ferrari2025], Garcia et al 2025[^garcia2025]). Values from other sources [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] pLDDT and PAE, and [[ProteinMPNN]] likelihoods correlates poorly with expression probability. None of these values consistently correlate with folding cooperativity (e.g., differences in unfolding dynamics).
