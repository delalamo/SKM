#### Summary

**A greater proportion of viral sequence closely co-cluster together than prokaryotic and eukaryotic sequences, and these sequences get lost during clustering** [@gurev2025]. For example, 90% clustering reduces prokaryotic/eukaryotic sequences 8-fold but viral sequences 550-fold.
