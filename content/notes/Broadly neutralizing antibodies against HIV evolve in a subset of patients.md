#### Summary
**[[Broadly neutralizing antibodies]] against HIV evolve in only a small number of patients, and usually in low titers** (Ovchinnikov et al 2018[^ovchinnikov2018]).

[^ovchinnikov2018]: Ovchinnikov et al. (2018) "Role of framework mutations and antibody flexibility in the evolution of broadly neutralizing antibodies." *eLife*. https://doi.org/10.7554/eLife.33038
