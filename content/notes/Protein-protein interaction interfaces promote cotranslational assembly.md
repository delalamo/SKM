#### Summary
 **[[Protein-protein interactions|Protein-protein interaction]] interfaces promote cotranslational assembly** [^badonyi2022]. This means that interaction surfaces are more common on the N-terminal part of proteins in the PDB.

[^badonyi2022]: Badonyi & Marsh (2022) "Large protein complex interfaces have evolved to promote cotranslational assembly." *eLife*. https://doi.org/10.7554/eLife.79602
