#### Summary

Increasing the number of diffusion samples from all-atom protein structure prediction methods is sufficient to increase the fraction of correctly docked poses ([10.64898__2026.03.02.709004|Smorodina et al 2026]). This is in contrast with conformational sampling, where several cases have been shown of biomolecular diffusion models being unable to reproduce equilibrium dynamics of biological systems.
