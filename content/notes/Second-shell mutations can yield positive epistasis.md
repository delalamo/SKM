#### Summary
 **Combinations of mutations in second-shell residues can yield positive (epistasis)** [@damry2021; @zarifi2025]. For example, phosphotriesterase H254R (8x better), when combined with D233E (2x better), has a 680-fold greater activity, whereas [[Kemp eliminase|Kemp eliminases]] with beneficial active site mutations tended to see greater activity boosts from second-shell mutations than those withouts.
