#### Summary
 **Proteins in halophilic organisms are more negatively charged, and consequently have a much lower isoelectric point than proteins in most model organisms** (mentioned by [@ding2024protein]). These can make them attractive starting points for [[Directed evolution|laboratory evolution]].
