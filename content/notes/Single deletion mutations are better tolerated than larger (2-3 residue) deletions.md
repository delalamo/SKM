#### Summary

**[[Variant effect prediction#Indels|Deletion mutations]] of 1 amino acid are better tolerated than deletion mutations of 2-3 AAs.** This is not true of insertions (Topolska et al 2023[^topolska2023]).

[^topolska2023]: Topolska et al. (2023) "Deep indel mutagenesis reveals the impact of amino acid insertions and deletions on protein stability and function." https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.10.06.561180
