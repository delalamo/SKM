#### Summary
**[[Autoimmunity]] involves the constant activation and reactivation of new [[B cells]], meaning that new pathogenic [[Antibodies|antibodies]] can be created at any point** (Robinson 2014[^robinson2014]). The severity of the disease correlates with the proportion of active antibodies circulating in blood.
