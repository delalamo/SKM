#### Summary
**[[Autoimmunity]] involves the constant activation and reactivation of new [[b-cells|B cells]], meaning that new pathogenic [[antibodies|antibodies]] can be created at any point** [@robinson2014]. The severity of the disease correlates with the proportion of active antibodies circulating in blood.
