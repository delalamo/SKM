#### Summary
 **Unfolded and nonfunctional isoforms of folded proteins are predicted as folded by [[Protein language models|PLM]]-based [[Structure prediction|protein folding neural networks]]** [@zhang2024]. [@pozo2021] first demonstrated that isoforms of well-folded proteins can be unstructured.
