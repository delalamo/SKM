#### Summary
**[[Low-rank Adaptation]] avoids [[Catastrophic forgetting]] but causes models to learn less than if they were subject to full fine-tuning** [^biderman2024]. In part this is due to strong regularization (relative to other methods such as weight decay or dropout).

- [[Fine-tuning can be detrimental to performance]]

[^biderman2024]: Biderman et al. (2024) "LoRA Learns Less and Forgets Less." https://doi.org/10.48550/ARXIV.2405.09673
