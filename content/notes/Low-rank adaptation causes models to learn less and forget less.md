#### Summary
**[[Low-rank Adaptation]] avoids [[Catastrophic forgetting|catastrophic forgetting]] but causes models to learn less than if they were subject to full fine-tuning** [@biderman2024]. In part this is due to strong regularization (relative to other methods such as weight decay or dropout).
