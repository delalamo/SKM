#### Summary
**The [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDR3]] loop of [[Nanobodies|nanobodies]] is far more likely to be part of the paratope than the CDRH3 of traditional heterodimeric [[tags/antibodies|antibodies]]** ([@gordon2023], De [@de2006]). Additionally, it comprises a larger part of the paratope than CDRH3 in antibodies.
