#### Summary
 **[[Protein backbone design|De novo protein backbones]] designed by [[Inversion of protein folding neural networks|hallucination]] are more likely to be recovered by [[Structure prediction|structure prediction neural networks]] than those designed by [[Diffusion models|diffusion]]** (such as RF-diffusion; Frank et al 2024[^frank2024]). This is particularly true of larger designs.
