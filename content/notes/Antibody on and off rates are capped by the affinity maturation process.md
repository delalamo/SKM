#### Summary
**[[antibodies|Antibody]] on and off rates are capped by the [[affinity-maturation|affinity maturation]] process** [@foote1995]. Authors propose caps of $10^{5}$ to $10^{6}\ M^{-1}s^{-1}$ for $k_{on}$ and $10^{-3}$ to $10^{-4}\ s^{-1}$ for $k_{off}$, leading to a cap of $10^{-10}\ M$ for $k_{d}$, beyond which [[b-cells|B cells]] would have no selective advantage. The on-rate cap is due to diffusion limits, while the off-rate is governed by antibody reabsorption and [[Endocytosis|endocytosis]].
