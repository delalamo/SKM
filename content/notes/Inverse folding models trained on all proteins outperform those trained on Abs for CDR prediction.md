#### Summary
 **[[inverse-folding|Inverse folding]] models trained on all protein structures, such as [[ProteinMPNN]], have high sequence recovery of residues on the [[Complementarity-determining regions#CDRH3|CDRH3]] loop of [[antibodies|antibodies]] that are near the antigen** ([[_Articles that need citations|citation needed]]). This could be because the data is too thin for antibody-specific models to figure this out (unlike other [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRs]]). However, best performance was obtained when fine-tuning general models with antibody structures.
