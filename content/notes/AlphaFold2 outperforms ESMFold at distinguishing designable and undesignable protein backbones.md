#### Summary
 **[[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] outperforms [[ESMFold]] at distinguishing designable and undesignable protein backbones** (Hermosilla et al 2023[^hermosilla2023]). ESMFold seems to want to fold everything, whereas AlphaFold will only fold sequences that actually fold. This is NOT true of non-beta-sheet membrane proteins (at least for AF).
