#### Summary
 **[[alphafold2|AlphaFold2]] outperforms [[ESMFold]] at distinguishing designable and undesignable protein backbones** [@hermosilla2023]. ESMFold seems to want to fold everything, whereas AlphaFold will only fold sequences that actually fold. This is NOT true of non-beta-sheet membrane proteins (at least for AF).
