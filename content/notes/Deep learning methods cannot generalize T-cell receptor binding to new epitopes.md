#### Summary

**Deep learning methods fail to identify new epitopes for [[T-cell receptors]].** They only generalize to very similar epitopes found in the training set (Groce et al 2023[^groce2023]). This could be applicable to [[B-cell receptors]] and [[Antibodies]] as well.
