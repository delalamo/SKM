#### Summary

**Most protein complexes, be they homo- or heterooligomers, are symmetric** [^schweke2023]. This is consistent with their mass prediction of homo-oligomers using [[AlphaFold2]].

[^schweke2023]: Schweke et al. (2024) "An atlas of protein homo-oligomerization across domains of life." Cell. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cell.2024.01.022
