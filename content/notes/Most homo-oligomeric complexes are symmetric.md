#### Summary

**Most protein complexes, be they homo- or heterooligomers, are symmetric** (Schweke et al 2023[^schweke2023]). This is consistent with their mass prediction of homo-oligomers using [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]].

[^schweke2023]: Schweke et al. (2023) "An atlas of protein homo-oligomerization across domains of life." https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.06.09.544317
