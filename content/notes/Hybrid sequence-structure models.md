**Hybrid sequence-structure models** combine [[protein-language-models|protein language models]] with [[inverse-folding|inverse folding]] models to draw from both massive sequence databases and highly detailed structural data.

* **MIF-ST**
* **ProstT5** [@heinzinger2023]
* **[[SaProt]]**
* **InstructPLM**
* **CarbonDesign** [@ren2024]
