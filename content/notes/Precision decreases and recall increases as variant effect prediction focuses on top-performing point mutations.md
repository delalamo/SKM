#### Summary

**Precision decreases and recall increases as [[tags/variant-effect-prediction|variant effect prediction]] focuses on top-performing point mutations** [@li2025]. In other words, most methods will correctly identify beneficial mutations, but will also predict that poor mutations are beneficial.
