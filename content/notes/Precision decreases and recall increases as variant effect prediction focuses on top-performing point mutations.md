#### Summary

**Precision decreases and recall increases as [[Variant effect prediction|variant effect prediction]] focuses on top-performing point mutations** (Li et al 2025b[^li2025]). In other words, most methods will correctly identify beneficial mutations, but will also predict that poor mutations are beneficial.
