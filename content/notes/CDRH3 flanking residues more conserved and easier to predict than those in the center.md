#### Summary
**Residues at the ends of [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] are more conserved than those near the center of the loop** [@guest2021]. As a result, they are easier to predict, as shown by Paul et al. [@paul2023an] and [@neyestanak2025].
