#### Summary

**Protein structures determined by X-ray crystallography can have unphysiological salt bridges.** Tomlinson et al 2009[^tomlinson2009] found that this was the case with lysines in the model protein GB1 using NMR:

> "the six surface-exposed lysines in protein G are not involved in significant salt bridge interactions, even though such interactions are found consistently in crystal structures"

[^tomlinson2009]: Tomlinson et al. (2009) "Characterization of Salt Bridges to Lysines in the Protein G B1 Domain." *Journal of the American Chemical Society*. https://doi.org/10.1021/ja808223p
