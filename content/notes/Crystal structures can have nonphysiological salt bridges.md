#### Summary

**Protein structures determined by X-ray crystallography can have unphysiological salt bridges.** [@tomlinson2009] found that this was the case with lysines in the model protein GB1 using NMR:

> "the six surface-exposed lysines in protein G are not involved in significant salt bridge interactions, even though such interactions are found consistently in crystal structures"
