#### Summary
 **Conformationally stabilizing mutations can be mined from sequence [[Clustering|clusters]] that, when used as a [[Multiple sequence alignments|multiple sequence alignment]] for [[tags/structure-prediction|structure prediction]] using [[tags/alphafold2|AlphaFold2]], yield the target conformation** [@waymentsteele2023]. Authors found three mutations this way that were enriched in clusters, introduced them into the WT of interest, and confirmed by NMR that the protein adopted the target fold.
