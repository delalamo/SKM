#### Summary
 **Conformationally stabilizing mutations can be mined from sequence [[Clustering|clusters]] that, when used as a [[Multiple sequence alignments|multiple sequence alignment]] for [[Structure prediction|structure prediction]] using [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]], yield the target conformation** [^waymentsteele2023]. Authors found three mutations this way that were enriched in clusters, introduced them into the WT of interest, and confirmed by NMR that the protein adopted the target fold.

[^waymentsteele2023]: Wayment-Steele et al. (2023) "Predicting multiple conformations via sequence clustering and AlphaFold2." *Nature*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-023-06832-9
