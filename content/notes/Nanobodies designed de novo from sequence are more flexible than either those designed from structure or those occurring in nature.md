#### Summary

**[[Nanobodies]] designed [[De novo antibody design|de novo]] from sequence (e.g., from [[Protein language models|PLMs]]) tend to be more flexible than those designed from structure, or those occurring naturally** (Löhr et al 2022[^lhr2022]).

[^lhr2022]: Löhr et al. (2022) "Conformational Entropy as a Potential Liability of Computationally Designed Antibodies." *Biomolecules*. https://doi.org/10.3390/biom12050718
