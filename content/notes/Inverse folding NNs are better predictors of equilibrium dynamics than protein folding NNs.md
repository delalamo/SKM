#### Summary
**[[Inverse folding]] NNs are better predictors of [[Protein dynamics|equilibrium dynamics]] than [[Structure prediction|protein folding NNs]]** [@cavanagh2025]. This was shown using [[BioEmu]], [[Alternate conformations can be sampled with MSA-based structure prediction methods using custom PDB databases and subsampled MSAs|AF-cluster]], [[ProteinMPNN]], [[ESM-IF]], ThermoMPNN, and Frame2Seq.
