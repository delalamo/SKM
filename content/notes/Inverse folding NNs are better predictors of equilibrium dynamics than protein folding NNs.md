#### Summary
**[[Inverse folding]] NNs are better predictors of [[Protein dynamics|equilibrium dynamics]] than [[Structure prediction|protein folding NNs]]** (Cavanagh et al 2025[^cavanagh2025]). This was shown using [[BioEmu]], [[Alternate conformations can be sampled with MSA-based structure prediction methods using custom PDB databases and subsampled MSAs|AF-cluster]], [[ProteinMPNN]], [[ESM-IF]], ThermoMPNN, and Frame2Seq.
