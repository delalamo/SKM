#### Summary
**[[Antibodies]] and [[Nanobodies|nanobodies]] with distinct [[Germline|V genes]] and [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] loops can convergently evolve to bind to the same epitope in the same place** ([^malby1994], De [^de2005]). In the case of the nanobodies identified by De Genst, both had the same D regions and the same non-germline mutations in CDR2.
