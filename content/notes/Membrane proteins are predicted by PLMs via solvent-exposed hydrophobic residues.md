#### Summary

**[[Attention (machine learning)|Attention matrices]] of [[tags/protein-language-models|PLMs]] can capture whether a protein sequence is interpreted as a soluble protein or membrane protein** [@wenzel2024]. In membrane proteins, more attention is given to solvent-exposed hydrophobic residues.
