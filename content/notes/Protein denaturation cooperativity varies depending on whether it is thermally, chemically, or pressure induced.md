#### Summary
 **Protein denaturation cooperativity varies depending on whether it is thermally, chemically, or pressure induced** (Seelig & Seelig 2024[^seelig2024]). Thermally induced unfolding showed the greatest cooperativity, followed by pressure then chemical (via guanidinium hydrochloride). However, pressure-based denaturation reduced protein volume, whereas thermal and chemical denaturation increased it. This was determined using apolipoprotein as a test case.

[^seelig2024]: Seelig & Seelig (2024) "Pressure protein denaturation compared to thermal and chemical unfolding - Analyses with cooperative models." https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.08.14.608019
