#### Summary
**Feynman-Kac steering** is a sequential Monte Carlo-based scheme for [[Diffusion guidance|guiding]] [[Diffusion models|diffusion models]] at inference time. Examples include its use in the [[Alphafold3]] clone [[Boltz]].
