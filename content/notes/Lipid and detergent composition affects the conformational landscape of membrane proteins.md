#### Summary
**The composition of lipids and/or detergents affects the [[Protein dynamics|conformational landscape]] of membrane proteins** [@hoffmann2024]. This was determined using [[cryo-EM]] on MsbA, which adopts a wide inward-open conformation in detergent micelles but a narrow inward-open conformation in lipid nanodiscs.
