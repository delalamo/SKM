The **heavy chains** of [[tags/antibodies|Antibodies]] contain both the [[Complementarity-determining regions#CDRH3|CDRH3]] loop mediating a disproportionate number of contacts with antigens, as well as larger [[Constant-regions|constant regions]]. They are also similar to [[Nanobodies]] (albeit larger).

#### Diversity

Heavy chains differ across different types of antibodies:
* IgA antibodies: $\alpha$-type heavy chain with three immunoglobulin domains
	* IgA1: $\alpha 1$
	* IgA2: $\alpha 2$
* IgD antibodies: $\delta$-type heavy chain with three immunoglobulin domains
* IgE antibodies: $\epsilon$-type heavy chain with four immunoglobulin domains
* IgG antibodies: $\gamma$-type heavy chain with three immunoglobulin domains
	* IgG1: $\gamma 1$
	* IgG2: $\gamma 2$
	* IgG3: $\gamma 3$
	* IgG4: $\gamma 4$
* IgM antibodies: $\mu$-type heavy chain with four immunoglobulin domains
