#### Summary
**Liu et al 2024d[^liu2024] (authors of [[Weight-decomposed low-rank adaptation|DoRA]]) found that [[Low-rank Adaptation]] changes the magnitude, but not direction, of matrices.** This observation prompted the development of [[Weight-decomposed low-rank adaptation|DoRA]].
