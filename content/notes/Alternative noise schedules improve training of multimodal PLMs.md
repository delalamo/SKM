#### Summary

**Alternative noise schedules improve training of [[Protein language models|protein language models]]** [@hayes2025]. No ablation data is provided for this claim, but authors of [[ESM]]3 state that training using a typical 15% masking rate yielded poor results relative to a more random noise schedule.
