#### Summary

**Alternative noise schedules improve training of [[Protein language models|protein language models]]** (Hayes et al 2025[^hayes2025]). No ablation data is provided for this claim, but authors of [[ESM]]3 state that training using a typical 15% masking rate yielded poor results relative to a more random noise schedule.

[^hayes2025]: Hayes et al. (2025) "Simulating 500 million years of evolution with a language model." *Science*. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.ads0018
