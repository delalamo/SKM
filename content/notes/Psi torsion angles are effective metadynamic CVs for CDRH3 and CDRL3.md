#### Summary
**[[Metadynamics]] [[MD simulations]] on [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] dynamics by [^fernandezquintero2020] used a "linear combination of sine and cosine of the psi torsion angles" as [[Collective variables]].** This was shown in a previous paper to capture conformational transitions comprehensively. In one study, the [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRL3]] loop was also included.

[^fernandezquintero2020]: Fernández-Quintero et al. (2020) "Local and Global Rigidification Upon Antibody Affinity Maturation." *Frontiers in Molecular Biosciences*. https://doi.org/10.3389/fmolb.2020.00182
