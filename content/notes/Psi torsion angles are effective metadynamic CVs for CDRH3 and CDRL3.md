#### Summary
**[[Metadynamics]] [[MD simulations]] on [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] dynamics by [@fernandezquintero2020local] used a "linear combination of sine and cosine of the psi torsion angles" as [[Collective variables]].** This was shown in a previous paper to capture conformational transitions comprehensively. In one study, the [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRL3]] loop was also included.
