#### Summary
**[[Kinked CDRH3 loops|Kinked]] [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDR3]] loops in [[Nanobodies|nanobodies]] are on average 3 to 4 residues longer than extended CDR3 loops** (Bahrami Dizicheh et al 2023[^bahrami2023]).

[^bahrami2023]: Bahrami Dizicheh et al. (2023) "VHH CDR-H3 conformation is determined by VH germline usage." *Communications Biology*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s42003-023-05241-y
