#### Summary
 **Small energy barriers (<4.5 $k_{B}T$) separate the folded and unfolded states of fast-folding proteins** (Lindorff-Larsen et al 2011[^lindorfflarsen2011]). In some proteins, the energy barriers are so low that it is difficult to say when protein folding ends and local relaxation begins.

[^lindorfflarsen2011]: Lindorff-Larsen et al. (2011) "How Fast-Folding Proteins Fold." *Science*. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1208351
