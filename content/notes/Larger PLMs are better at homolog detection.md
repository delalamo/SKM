#### Summary

**Larger [[Protein language models|PLMs]] are better at detecting distant homologs** [^wu2024]. This is in contrast with the observation that larger models are generally no better at most tasks ([[Protein property prediction using PLMs does not benefit from scale except when predicting inferring features of either structural or sparsely populated sequence families]]).
