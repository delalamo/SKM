#### Summary
 **Including ligands during [[tags/inverse-folding|inverse folding]] allows amino acids in the binding pocket to be recovered at higher rates** [@dauparas2023]. For one test case, two of the three residues in a catalytic triad were not correctly predicted without ligand. However, inclusion of the ligand caused all three to be designed correctly.
