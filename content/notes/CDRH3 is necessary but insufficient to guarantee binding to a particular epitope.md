#### Summary
**Identical [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] is necessary but insufficient to guarantee binding to the same epitope** (D'Angelo et al 2018[^dangelo2018]). This was determined after observing that many antibodies with different combinations of V, D, and J genes but the same CDRH3 are nonetheless unable to bind to the same target.

[^dangelo2018]: D’Angelo et al. (2018) "Many Routes to an Antibody Heavy-Chain CDR3: Necessary, Yet Insufficient, for Specific Binding." *Frontiers in Immunology*. https://doi.org/10.3389/fimmu.2018.00395
