#### Summary
**Identical [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]] is necessary but insufficient to guarantee binding to the same epitope** (D'[@dangelo2018]). This was determined after observing that many antibodies with different combinations of V, D, and J genes but the same CDRH3 are nonetheless unable to bind to the same target.
