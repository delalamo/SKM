#### Summary
**[[AlphaFold|AlphaFold3]]-generation methods like Boltz and Chai exclusively incorporate [[Multiple sequence alignments|MSA]] information into the pair representation, in contrast with [[Evoformer]]-based methods that reason over the information in the folding trunk** [^abramson2024].

[^abramson2024]: Abramson et al. (2024) "Accurate structure prediction of biomolecular interactions with AlphaFold 3." *Nature*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-024-07487-w
