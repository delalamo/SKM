#### Summary
**[[alphafold3|AlphaFold3]]-generation methods like Boltz and Chai exclusively incorporate [[Multiple sequence alignments|MSA]] information into the pair representation, in contrast with [[Evoformer]]-based methods that reason over the information in the folding trunk** [@abramson2024].
