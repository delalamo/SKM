#### Summary
**Half of all disease-causing [[Evolution and natural selection#Mutations|missense mutations]] are due to disruption of [[Stability and thermostability|protein stability]]** [^cagiada2024]. Authors find that a smaller fraction were destabilizing in [[Intrinsically disordered regions]]. This was evaluated using (ESM)-1b, [[ESM-IF]] with cutoffs set using the mega-scale[^megascale] dataset.
