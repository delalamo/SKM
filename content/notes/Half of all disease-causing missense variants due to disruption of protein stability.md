#### Summary
**Half of all disease-causing [[evolution-and-natural-selection#Mutations|missense mutations]] are due to disruption of [[thermostability|protein stability]]** [@cagiada2024]. Authors find that a smaller fraction were destabilizing in [[Intrinsically disordered regions]]. This was evaluated using (ESM)-1b, [[ESM-IF]] with cutoffs set using the mega-scale [@tsuboyama2023] dataset.
