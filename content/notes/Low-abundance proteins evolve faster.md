#### Summary
**Proteins that are low-abundance evolve faster** (Pál et al 2001[^pl2001]). This is true at both the sequence and structural level (Lemke et al 2025[^lemke2025]). Exceptions include "suicide enzymes" such as those involved in thiamine synthesis.
