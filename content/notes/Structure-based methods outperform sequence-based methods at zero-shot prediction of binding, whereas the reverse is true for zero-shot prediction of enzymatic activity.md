#### Summary

**Structure-based methods, such as [[Inverse folding|inverse folding models]], outperform sequence-based methods such as [[Protein language models|protein language models]] and [[Potts models]] at zero-shot [[Stability and thermostability#Prediction|binding prediction]], whereas the reverse is true for zero-shot [[Fitness prediction|prediction of enzymatic activity]]** (Li et al 2024c[^li2024]).
