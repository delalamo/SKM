#### Summary
**The process of [[Antibody humanization|humanization]] can cause [[antibodies|Antibodies]] to become less stable** [@tennenhouse2023]. Humanization can also destabilize specific [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRs]], leading to failure during humanization [@fernandezquintero2019].

#### Details
Bahrami [@bahrami2023] give an example of a [[Nanobodies|nanobody]] that had its framework changed such that its [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDR3]] conformation changed from [[Kinked CDRH3 loops|kinked]] to extended. In the process its [[thermostability|thermostability]] dropped by 10°C.
