**Protein function prediction** is the prediction of what proteins do from sequence or structure alone. Recent methods rely on embeddings from [[tags/protein-language-models|PLMs]], [[tags/inverse-folding|inverse folding]] or [[Hybrid sequence-structure models|hybrid sequence/structure models]].
