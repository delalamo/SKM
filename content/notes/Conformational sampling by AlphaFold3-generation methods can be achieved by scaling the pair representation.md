#### Summary

**Conformational sampling can be achieved by AlphaFold3-generation structure prediction methods by scaling the pair representation** [^suzuki2026]. This is the part of the network that receives information from multiple sequence alignments, and is therefore somewhat equivalent to MSA subsampling.

[^suzuki2026]: Suzuki & Amagasa (2026) "Steering Conformational Sampling in Boltz-2 via Pair Representation Scaling." https://doi.org/10.64898/2026.01.23.701250
