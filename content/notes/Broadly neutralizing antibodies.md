**Broadly neutralizing [[tags/antibodies|antibodies]]** are antibodies that can bind to multiple isotypes of the same protein, e.g., against different variants of the same viral protein for example. [[Conformational entropy in antibodies decreases during affinity maturation|Unlike antibodies]], they do not appear to be more rigid than their corresponding [[Germline|germline]].

#### Details
* Most broadly neutralizing antibodies against hepatitis C have been isolated from chronically ill patients
