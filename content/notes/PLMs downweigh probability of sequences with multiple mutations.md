## Summary

**[[Protein language models|PLMs]] are biased against sequences with multiple mutations** ([Shaw et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.09.28.560044)).

## Details

The authors propose normalizing these by generating large quantities ($10^{4}$) of mutants with equal number of mutations and ranking them that way.
