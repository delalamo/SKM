## Summary

**Protein [[Stability and thermostability|stability]], expression, and resistance to proteolysis are often correlated.** This is cited by [Teufl et al 2022][^teufl2022]. A community assessment of PROSS also found correlation between stability and expression ([Peleg et al 2021][^peleg2021]). That has also been observed in [[Antibodies|antibodies]], with lower expression levels and [[Antibody thermostability decreases during affinity maturation|thermostabilities]] following [[Somatic hypermutation]] ([Shehata et al 2019][^shehata2019]). The link between stability and resistance to proteolysis has been described by [Tzul et al 2017][^tzul2017], [Tsuboyama et al 2023][^tsuboyama2023], and [Diaz et al 2023][^diaz2023].
