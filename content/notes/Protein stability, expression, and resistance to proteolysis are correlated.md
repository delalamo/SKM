## Summary

**Protein [[Stability and thermostability|stability]], expression, and resistance to proteolysis are often correlated.** This is cited by [Teufl et al 2022](https://doi.org/10.1021/acssynbio.1c00512). A community assessment of PROSS also found correlation between stability and expression ([Peleg et al 2021](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jmb.2021.166964)). That has also been observed in [[Antibodies|antibodies]], with lower expression levels and [[Antibody thermostability decreases during affinity maturation|thermostabilities]] following [[Somatic hypermutation]] ([Shehata et al 2019](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.celrep.2019.08.056)). The link between stability and resistance to proteolysis has been described by [Tzul et al 2017](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1613892114), [Tsuboyama et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-023-06328-6), and [Diaz et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.05.15.540857).
