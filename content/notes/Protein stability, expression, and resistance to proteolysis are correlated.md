## Summary

**Protein [[Stability and thermostability|stability]], expression, and resistance to proteolysis are often correlated.** This is cited by [@teufl2022]. A community assessment of PROSS also found correlation between stability and expression [@peleg2021]. That has also been observed in [[Antibodies|antibodies]], with lower expression levels and [[Antibody thermostability decreases during affinity maturation, up to a threshold|thermostabilities]] following [[Somatic hypermutation]] [@shehata2019]. The link between stability and resistance to proteolysis has been described by [@tzul2017], [@tsuboyama2023], and [@diaz2023].
