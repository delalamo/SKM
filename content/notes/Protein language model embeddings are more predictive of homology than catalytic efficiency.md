#### Summary
 **[[Protein language models|Protein language model]] embeddings are more predictive of homology than catalytic efficiency** (Muir et al 2024[^muir2024]). In fact, [[ESM]] embeddings were slightly better than random and worse than one-hot encodings at identifying highly active adenylate kinase variants.
