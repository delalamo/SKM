#### Summary
 **[[protein-language-models|Protein language model]] embeddings are more predictive of homology than catalytic efficiency** [@muir2024]. In fact, [[ESM]] embeddings were slightly better than random and worse than one-hot encodings at identifying highly active adenylate kinase variants.
