#### Summary
**Masked [[Protein language models|PLMs]] (such as [[ESM]]) are more sensitive to training imbalances than autoregressive models (such as [[ProGen]];** Cheng et al 2024[^cheng2024]). Presumably this is also true of [[Transformer|transformers]] trained on natural language.
