#### Summary
**Novel functions can be [[Directed evolution|evolved in a laboratory]] in [[Amino-acid-polyamine-organocation-family|APC superfamily]] transporters via a single [[Variant effect prediction#Missense mutations|missense mutation]] in the permeation pathway** [^karapanagioti2023]. This was found to occur either by improving weak baseline activity Agp1 or establishing activity for new substrate Put4. These came with a slight fitness cost in the original substrates.

[^karapanagioti2023]: Karapanagioti et al. (2023) "Fitness landscape of substrate-adaptive mutations in evolved APC transporters." https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.10.12.562049
