#### Summary
**Most point mutations that [[Antibodies|antibodies]] obtain are the result of single-nucleotide changes.** [@kirby2025] noted that in the 51 somatic mutations observed across twelve antibodies against [[SARS-CoV-2]], 47 (92%) were single-nucleotide changes.
