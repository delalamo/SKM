#### Summary
**The BLOSUM62 matrix has been used to restrict mutagenesis of [[Antibodies|antibodies]]** (doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-31457-3), **and its usefulness varies by molecule**. BLOSUM-guided mutagenesis has been shown to lead to greater binding likelihood than [[Protein language models|protein language models]] and [[Inverse folding|inverse folding models]] (doi.org/10.1101/2024.03.26.586756) in [[Trastuzumab]]. Another report showed the opposite result[^li2023]. It may come down to the level of optimization already present in the starting sequence.
