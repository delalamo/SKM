#### Summary
**The BLOSUM62 matrix has been used to restrict mutagenesis of [[tags/antibodies|antibodies]]** [@makowski2022], **and its usefulness varies by molecule**. BLOSUM-guided mutagenesis has been shown to lead to greater binding likelihood than [[tags/protein-language-models|protein language models]] and [[tags/inverse-folding|inverse folding models]] [@chinery2024] in [[Trastuzumab]]. Another report showed the opposite result [@li2023machine]. It may come down to the level of optimization already present in the starting sequence.
