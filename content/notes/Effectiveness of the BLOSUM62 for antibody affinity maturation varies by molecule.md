#### Summary
**The BLOSUM62 matrix has been used to restrict mutagenesis of [[Antibodies|antibodies]]** [^makowski2022], **and its usefulness varies by molecule**. BLOSUM-guided mutagenesis has been shown to lead to greater binding likelihood than [[Protein language models|protein language models]] and [[Inverse folding|inverse folding models]] [^chinery2024] in [[Trastuzumab]]. Another report showed the opposite result[^li2023]. It may come down to the level of optimization already present in the starting sequence.
