#### Summary
**Trimerization domains are often used to replace the metastable membrane-spanning regions of [[Viral-fusion-proteins]]. They have other uses as well.**

#### Examples
* **Foldon**: From fibritin C-terminus of bacteriophage-T4. Used in vaccines against [[SARS-CoV-1]], [[SARS-CoV-2]] ([[Spike protein]]), MPV, RSV
* **Collagen**: ??
* **Aldolase**: ??
* **GCN4** AKA "isoleucine zipper" from yeast: Used in Hendra virus F-protein, Nipah virus F-protein, Influenza Hemagglutinin (HA)
