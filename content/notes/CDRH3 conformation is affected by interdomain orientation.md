#### Summary
**The orientation between the heavy and light chains of [[Antibodies|antibodies]] can affect the conformation of [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRH3]]** (Fernandez-Quintero et al 2023[^fernandez2023]).

[^fernandez2023]: Fernandez-Quintero et al. (2023) "Assessing antibody and nanobody nativeness for hit selection and humanization with AbNatiV." *mAbs*. https://doi.org/10.1080/19420862.2023.2175319
