#### Summary
 **Natural human [[tags/antibodies|Antibodies]] have a [[tags/thermostability|thermostability]] threshold that is usually reached within a few mutations during [[tags/affinity-maturation|Affinity maturation]].** Beyond this, no further decreases are observed [@shehata2019]. Note that thermostability is necessary but insufficient for expression [@tharp2026], as correct folding and trafficking is also required.
