#### Summary
**[[Heavy chains|Heavy]] and [[Light chains|light chains]] of [[Single chain variable fragments|scFvs]] with the same [[Framework region|framework]] and antigen-binding mode can be exchanged to increase screening diversity** (Bennett et al 2024[^bennett2024]). This was not screened *in vitro* but was observed using [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold3]] filtering.
