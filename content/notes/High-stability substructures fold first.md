#### Summary
**High-[[Stability and thermostability|stability]] substructures fold first** (Lindorff-Larsen et al 2011[^lindorfflarsen2011]). In a series of protein folding [[MD simulations]], individual helices that are most stable in the folded state are the first to form.

[^lindorfflarsen2011]: Lindorff-Larsen et al. (2011) "How Fast-Folding Proteins Fold." *Science*. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1208351
