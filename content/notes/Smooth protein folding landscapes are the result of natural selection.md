#### Summary
 **Smooth [[Protein folding|protein folding]] landscapes are the result of [[Evolution and natural selection|natural selection]]** (Watters et al 2007[^watters2007]). This was the conclusion after observing that [[De novo designed proteins have multiphasic folding pathways|the de novo designed protein Top7 has a multiphasic folding pathway]].
