#### Summary
**Subnetworks within protein language models encode specific protein families** [^journalp]. This is a key finding in the field.

A related result shows that sequence embeddings capture evolutionary information [^s]. Additionally, inverse folding is possible at scale [^ref2022].

Multiple citations can appear together: models trained on sequences alone capture structural information [^bioinfor] and can be fine-tuned for downstream tasks.
