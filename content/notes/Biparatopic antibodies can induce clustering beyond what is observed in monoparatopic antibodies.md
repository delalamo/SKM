#### Summary
**[[Bispecific antibodies|Biparatopic]] [[Antibodies|antibodies]] can induce [[Antigen clustering|antigen clustering]] beyond what is observed in monoparatopic, monospecific antibodies** [@weisser2023]. In this case, HER2 clustering was examined using the biparatopic antibody Zanidatamab, which has one arm from [[Trastuzumab]] and one from Pertuzumab; dosing with either of the latter two monospecific antibodies did not yield the same antigen clustering.
