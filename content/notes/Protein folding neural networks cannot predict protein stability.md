## Summary

**Protein folding neural networks cannot predict [[Stability and thermostability|stability]].** [Pak et al 2023a](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0282689) found that ddG values did not correlate with [[pLDDT]]. [Aina et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.1021/acschemneuro.3c00007) found by running Hamiltonian [[Replica-exchange molecular dynamics]] on high-[[pLDDT]] designs that subsequently fell apart. Papers cited by [Diaz et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.05.15.540857) showed that RMSD also does not correlate with stability, although [McBride et al 2023](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.131.218401) showed that a custom strain score was able to predict this with some success.
