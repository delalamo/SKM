#### Summary

**[[ESM-IF]], but not other [[tags/inverse-folding|inverse folding models]], has learned some evolutionary constraints from sequence databases** [@li2024c]. This is evidenced by a dependence on the availability of training data that is comparable to that of [[tags/protein-language-models|protein language models]] and [[Potts models]].
