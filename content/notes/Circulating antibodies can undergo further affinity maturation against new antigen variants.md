#### Summary
**Circulating [[Antibodies|antibodies]] can undergo further [[Affinity maturation|affinity maturation]] against new circulating antigen variants** [^tan2014]. This was shown with antibodies that were elicited against [[Influenza]] [[Vaccines|vaccines]].
