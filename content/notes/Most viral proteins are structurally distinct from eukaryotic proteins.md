#### Summary
 **About ~60% of proteins from viral genomes are structurally distinct from those found in eukaryotic genomes** [^nomburg2024]. This was determined using structures predicted with [[AlphaFold2]] and [[Foldseek]].

[^nomburg2024]: Nomburg et al. (2024) "Birth of protein folds and functions in the virome." *Nature*. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-024-07809-y
