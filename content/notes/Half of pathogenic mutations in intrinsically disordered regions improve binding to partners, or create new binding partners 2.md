#### Summary

Of ~350 screened mutations in the intrinsically disordered regions of 80 proteins shown to be pathogenic, half were shown to either enhance protein-protein interactions or create new interactions, while the other half shown to diminish binding ([10.1101__2023.09.18.558189|Kliche et al 2023]).
