#### Summary

**Sequences designed by [[protein-language-models|protein language models]] have the same distribution of [[plddt|pLDDT]] values, sequence lengths, and *in vitro* activity as naturally occurring sequences** [@stocco2024]. This was measured in amylases and can be overcome with fine-tuning using [[Reinforcement learning|direct preference optimization]].
