#### Summary

Fitness prediction using inverse folding can be improved by including B-factor prediction and SASA prediction as auxiliary losses ([10.1021__acs.jcim.4c00036|Zhou et al 2024]). However, the optimal balance for these still favors the majority of the loss focused on cross-entropy loss of residue identity.
