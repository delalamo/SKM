#### Summary
**[[tags/epistasis|Epistasis]] in [[tags/antibodies|antibodies]] during [[tags/affinity-maturation|affinity maturation]] can be driven by conformational rearrangements in [[Complementarity-determining regions|CDRs]]** [@tharp2026]. In this case, the mechanism by which some mutations are not uniformly beneficial is due to [[Conformational entropy in antibodies decreases during affinity maturation|conformational rigidification]].
