**Axe-like [[Complementarity-determining regions#CDRH3|CDRH3]] loops** are those that are adopt a double-bent conformation ending with a beta sheet, and share features of both [[Kinked CDRH3 loops|kinked]] and unkinked CDRH3 loops. These are found in some [[Broadly neutralizing antibodies|broadly neutralizing antibodies]] and can be predicted with some confidence (doi.org/10.1101/2024.10.04.616739). An important sequence motif is D105, D106, G113, S114, G115 (presumably IMGT numbering), although this was only determined in silico using structure prediction.
