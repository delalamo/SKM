**Evo-velocity** is a method that simulates [[Evolution and natural selection|evolution]] using [[Protein language models|PLMs]] [[Sequence perplexity|pseudoperplexity]] values [@hie2022]. It was tested on numerous viral and eukaryotic proteins and predicted fitness trajectories align reasonably well with improvements in fitness. However, [@ektefaie2025] argue that PLMs are unable to reason about evolution.
