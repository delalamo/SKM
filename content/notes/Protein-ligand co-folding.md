**Protein-ligand co-folding** refers to methods that can [[structure-prediction|predict the structures of proteins]] with small molecules or other macromolecules such as RNA.

#### Methods

* [[alphafold3|AlphaFold3]]
* [[rosettafold|RosettaFold-AllAtom]]
* Chai-1
* Boltz-1
* NeuralPlexer
* Protenix
