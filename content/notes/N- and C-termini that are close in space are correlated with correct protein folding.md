#### Summary
 **Proteins with N- and C-termini that are close in space are correlated with correct [[Protein folding|protein folding]]** [^krishna2005]. Proteins with termini far apart fold with non-two-state kinetics.

[^krishna2005]: Krishna & Englander (2005) "The N-terminal to C-terminal motif in protein folding and function." *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences*. https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.0409114102
