#### Summary

High-pLDDT designs, particularly those generated by hallucination, can be insoluble ([@dauparas2022; @wang2022]). [@goverde2023] found that inversion of AlphaFold2 led to many surface-exposed hydrophobic residues, and that manual post-design intervention led to in vitro expression of some designs. Alternatively, inverse folding can circumvent this (see [[Inverse folding methods outperform sequence design by hallucination]]).
