#### Summary
 **Protein abundance is inversely correlated with its propensity to form nonfunctional [[tags/protein-protein-interactions|protein-protein interactions]]** [@levy2012]. This is more evident in *E. coli* than *S. cerivisiae* and *H. sapiens*. Note that this analysis was carried out in well-folded proteins in the PDB. This may be one of the constraints on high-abundance proteins that causes [[Low-abundance proteins evolve faster|low-abundance proteins to evolve faster]].
