#### Summary
 **Protein abundance is inversely correlated with its propensity to form nonfunctional [[Protein-protein interactions|protein-protein interactions]]** [^levy2012]. This is more evident in *E. coli* than *S. cerivisiae* and *H. sapiens*. Note that this analysis was carried out in well-folded proteins in the PDB.

[^levy2012]: Levy et al. (2012) "Cellular crowding imposes global constraints on the chemistry and evolution of proteomes." *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences*. https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1209312109
