#### Summary
 **[[Ribosomes]] penalize the unfolded states of proteins during [[tags/protein-folding|protein folding]] in a sequence-independent manner** [@streit2024]. This is achieved by physically expanding the conformational ensemble such that it is less spherical, reducing the entropic penalty to protein folding by 2 kcal/mol at 298 K and the SASA penalty by 11±4 kcal/mol.
