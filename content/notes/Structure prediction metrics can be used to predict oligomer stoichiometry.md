#### Summary

**Protein [[Structure prediction]] quality metrics, namely [[TM-score|pTM]], can be used to predict the stoichiometry of oligomers** (Liu et al 2025[^liu2025]). Adding PDB templates further improved prediction performance.

[^liu2025]: Liu et al. (2025) "Accurate Prediction of Protein Complex Stoichiometry by Integrating AlphaFold3 and Template Information." https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.01.12.632663
