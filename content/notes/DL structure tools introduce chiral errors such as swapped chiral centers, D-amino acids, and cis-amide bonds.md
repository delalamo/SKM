#### Summary
**Deep learning [[tags/structure-prediction|structure prediction]] tools introduce errors such as swapped chiral centers, steric clashes, and cis-amide bonds** [@abramson2024; @fernandezquintero2023]. [[tags/diffusion-models|Diffusion]]-based structure prediction methods are able to overcome this with [[tags/diffusion-guidance|guidance]].

#### Details
Antibody-specific tools include AbLooper, DeepAb, [[IgFold]], [[ImmuneBuilder]], and MOE homology modeler. Only DeepAb and ImmuneBuilder did not introduce D-amino acids.
