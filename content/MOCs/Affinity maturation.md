**Affinity maturation** is a process by which [[Antibodies|antibodies]] are iteratively improved *in vivo*. Described as "hyper-darwinian".

#### How antibody structures change during affinity maturation

* **Affinity maturation causes "Ab–Ag interfaces \[to be\] more similar to [[Protein-protein interactions|protein–protein interfaces]] in general"** ([[10.1111__febs.12597|Burkovitz et al 2013]]).

#### Miscellaneous

* **[[Affinity maturation]] changes association and dissociation rates of [[Antibodies]].** Some scientists found that SPR measurements found that mature antibodies had greater associate rates, while others looking at [[Broadly neutralizing antibodies]] saw a decrease in dissociation rate with no change in association rate (sources cited in intro of [[10.3389__fimmu.2018.00413|Jeliazkov et al 2018]]).
