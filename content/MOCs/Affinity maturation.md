**Affinity maturation** is a process by which [[Antibodies|antibodies]] are iteratively improved *in vivo*. Described as "hyper-darwinian".

## Somatic Hypermutation

- [[Antibodies from different repertoires within a given species targeting the same epitope often have the same light chain isotype and share critical residues]]
- [[DNA substitutions during somatic hypermutation are spatially clustered]]
