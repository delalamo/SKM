**OmegaFold** is a [[Structure prediction|protein structure prediction]] method from [[10.1101__2022.07.21.500999|Wu et al 2022]] that uses a custom-designed [[Protein language models|masked PLM]] to predict structures without [[Multiple sequence alignments]]. Similar to [[ESMFold]]. Found to model [[Complementarity-determining regions#CDRH3|CDRH3]] loops more accurately than [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] ([[10.1101__2023.04.26.538476|Singh et al 2023]]).
