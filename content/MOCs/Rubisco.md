**Ribulose 1,5-bisphosphate carboxylase** (also called **Rubisco**) is an enzyme that is used by plants and cyanobacteria to fix CO2. It a common target for [[Directed evolution|directed evolution]] due to its intrinsically low catalytic activity ([[10.1101__2025.01.19.633714|de Pins et al 2025]]).
