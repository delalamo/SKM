**RosettaFold** (sometimes stylized as RoseTTAfold) is a protein [[Structure prediction|structure prediction]] method unveiled in mid-2021 ([[10.1126__science.abj8754|Baek et al 2021]]). A second version, RosettaFold2, was released in early 2023 ([[10.1101__2023.05.24.542179|Baek et al 2023]]). Its architecture closely tracks that of [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]], with several changes, such as the use of an [[SE3-transformer]].
