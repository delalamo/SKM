**Immune repertoires** are the full breadth of B-cell and T-cell receptors being expressed by a human that are available for potential antigen binding. [[10.1080__19420862.2020.1729683|Rees 2020]] provides estimates suggesting that humans have naive repertoires of about $10^{9}$ sequences.
