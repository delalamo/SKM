**ESM-IF** is an [[Inverse folding]] method that uses twelve million [[AlphaFold2]] structures during training, in addition to PDB. It uses an encoder-decoder [[Transformer]] architecture with a [[Geometric Vector Perceptrons|GVP]] as well as fixed-order decoding (autoregressive).
