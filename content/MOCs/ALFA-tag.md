**ALFA-tag** is a sequence used in [[Protein engineering|protein engineering]], namely [[Membrane proteins can be bulked up for cryo-EM by inserting designed domains, including those with predefined epitopes for antibodies|bulking up membrane proteins for cryo-EM]]  ([[10.1038__s41467-019-12301-7|Götzke et al 2019]]). It reliably forms an alpha helix in solution with an epitope capable of binding [[Nanobodies|nanobodies]]. Prolines break the helix at either end.
