**Enhanced sampling** refers to methods to augment [[MD simulations]] by sample the [[Protein dynamics|conformational space of proteins]] more efficiently. In general there are two approaches:
1. Adding biases that drive simulations away from or toward certain end states, usually along pre-specified [[Collective variables|collective variables]] (e.g., [[Metadynamics|metadynamics]]). Per [[10.1021__acs.jctc.3c00290|Vani et al 2023a]] this is useful for crossing enthalpic barriers.
2. Splitting/stratifying simulations into multiple trajectories (e.g., [[Replica-exchange molecular dynamics|replica-exchange]]). Per [[10.1021__acs.jctc.3c00290|Vani et al 2023a]] this is useful for crossing entropic barriers.
