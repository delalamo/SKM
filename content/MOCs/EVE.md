EVE is a [[Variational autoencoders|VAE]]-based generative method for [[Variant effect prediction]] prediction that is trained on [[Multiple sequence alignments|MSAs]] ([[10.1038__s41586-021-04043-8|Frazer et al 2021]]).

\![[Pasted-image-20240502090757.png]]
*Figure from [[10.1038__s41586-021-04043-8|Frazer et al 2021]]*

#### Variations

* **EVEscape** uses EVE results, solvent accessibility, and hydrophobicity/charge changes to predict the probability of specific amino acids to occur during [[Antibodies|antibody]] escape of viral proteins ([[10.1038__s41586-023-06617-0|Thadani et al 2023]]). It was spplied to the [[Spike protein]] of [[SARS-CoV-2]].
