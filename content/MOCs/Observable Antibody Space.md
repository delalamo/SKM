The **Observable Antibody Space** (OAS) is a dataset of billions of [[Antibodies|antibody]] sequences and is widely used to train [[Antibody language models|antibody language models]]. It was first published by [[10.4049__jimmunol.1800708|Kovaltsuk et al 2018]].
