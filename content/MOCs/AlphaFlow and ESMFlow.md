**AlphaFlow** and **ESMFlow** are versions of [[AlphaFold|AlphaFold2]] and [[ESMFold]] that are fine-tuned on [[MD simulations]] to model conformational heterogeneity using [[Normalizing flows|normalizing flows]] ([[yQcebEgQfH|Jing et al 2023b]]).
