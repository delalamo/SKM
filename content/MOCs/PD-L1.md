**Programmed death ligand 1** (abbreviated **PD-L1** and also called **CD274**), is a 40 kDa transmembrane protein that suppressed the adaptive immune system by binding to [[PD-1]] receptors on [[T cells]] and preventing activation. This is a strategy used during pregnancy, but is also frequently used by tumor cells to avoid the immune system. It is a therapeutic target for [[Antibodies|antibodies]] (immunotherapy).
