**Grokking** refers to training a neural network far beyond overfitting. [[10.48550__ARXIV.2405.15071|Wang et al 2024]] showed that it can improve reasoning abilities of [[Transformer|transformers]], whereas [[10.48550__ARXIV.2503.19206|Springer et al 2025]] showed [[Overtrained language models are more difficult to fine-tune|it can make models more difficult to fine-tune]].
