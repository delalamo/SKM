**Geometric Vector Perceptrons**, or GVPs, are a type of neural network layer commonly combined with [[Graph neural networks]] for use on [[Inverse folding]] problems ([[10.48550__arXiv.2009.01411|Jing et al 2021]]). It was also used by [[ESM-IF]] ([[10.1101__2022.04.10.487779|Hsu et al 2022]]).

#### Notes
[[10.1093__protein__gzad015|Yang et al 2023c]] found that GVP-GNN achieved greater sequence recovery than standard GNNs of the same size with the same test set, but did not show improvements in [[Variant effect prediction|downstream prediction]].
