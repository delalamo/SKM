**Collective variables** (CVs) are low-dimensional mappings of protein/macromolecular movements, almost always in the context of [[MD simulations]]. Typically treated as a function $\xi$, with the lower-dimensional representation $\boldsymbol{z}$ being the result: $\boldsymbol{z} = \xi (\boldsymbol{x})$, where $\boldsymbol{x}$ is the positions of 3D coordinates.
