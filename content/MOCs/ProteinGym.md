**ProteinGym** is a dataset presented formally by [[10.1101__2023.12.07.570727|Notin et al 2023]] but used for several years previously, that aggregates deep mutational scanning data and ΔΔG data (e.g., from [[10.1038__s41586-023-06328-6|Tsuboyama et al 2023]]) for evaluation of various deep learning models.
