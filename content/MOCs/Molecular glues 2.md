**Molecular glues** are small molecules that facilitate [[Protein-protein interactions|protein-protein interactions]]. Most but not all of these act through [[Targeted protein degradation|targeted protein degradation]] ([[10.1038__s41573-021-00371-6|Békés et al 2022]]).
