**Evo-velocity** is a method that simulates [[Evolution and natural selection|evolution]] using [[Protein language models|PLMs]] [[Sequence perplexity|pseudoperplexity]] values ([[10.1016__j.cels.2022.01.003|Hie et al 2022]]). It was tested on numerous viral and eukaryotic proteins and predicted fitness trajectories align reasonably well with improvements in fitness. However, [[10.1101__2025.01.17.633626|Ektefaie et al 2025]] argue that PLMs are unable to reason about evolution.
