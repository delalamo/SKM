**Glycosylation** refers to the post-translation addition of sugars to amino acids. [[Antibodies]] are naturally glycosylated but as therapeutics are sensitive to the addition of more sugar groups. N-linked glycosylation sites include NG and N-!P-S/T-N where (!P is not Pro) (doi.org/10.1038/s41551-023-01079-1), while O-linked glycosylation sites include T-V-X-P and S/T-P-X-P where X is anything** (doi.org/10.1016/s0006-3495(01)76074-2).
