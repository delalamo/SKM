**RosettaFold** (sometimes stylized as RoseTTAfold) is a protein [[tags/structure-prediction|structure prediction]] method unveiled in mid-2021 [@baek2021]. A second version, RosettaFold2, was released in early 2023 [@baek2023]. Its architecture closely tracks that of [[tags/alphafold2|AlphaFold2]], with several changes, such as the use of an [[SE3-transformer]].
