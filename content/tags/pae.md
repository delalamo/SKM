**Predicted aligned error** (PAE) is a measurement calculated by protein [[tags/structure-prediction|structure prediction]] neural networks to capture positional errors between two amino acids in a computational model. It was introduced by [[tags/alphafold2|AlphaFold2]]. A derivative metric, ipSAE, has been shown to be more robust at identifying potential binders.
