#### Summary
**High-confidence predictions (assessed with [[pLDDT]]) from [[Protein language models|protein language models]] co-cluster together in embedding space and their placement therein correlates with performance on [[Variant effect prediction|variant effect prediction]] tasks** (doi.org/10.1038/s41592-026-03028-7). In contrast, low-confidence predictions fall in a "junkyard".
