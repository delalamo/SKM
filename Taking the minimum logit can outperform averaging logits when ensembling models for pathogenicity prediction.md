#### Summary
Using the minimum logit from an ensemble of models rather than ensemble all logits can lead to improved performance on [[Variant effect prediction|pathogenicity prediction]] (doi.org/10.1038/s41592-026-03050-9). This is particularly effective when distinct models have different levels of confidence.
